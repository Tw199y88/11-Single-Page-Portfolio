About Me

Hello, I'm Brooke Anderson, currently pursuing a degree in UX at Utah Valley University. My passion lies in crafting designs that prioritize user-friendly experiences. With 5 years of experience in web design and SEO, I'm excited to transition into this new phase of my career!

Throughout my journey, I've had the privilege of empowering small businesses across Utah Valley to thrive online. By delivering web design, custom applications, and marketing materials at an affordable cost, I've contributed to their success.

Working alongside my partner in crime, Cody Anderson, who happens to be my husband and a skilled developer, we collaborate to create beautiful and intuitive designs. Beyond work, you'll often find me tidying up at home or immersing myself in the great outdoors while camping.

I'm deeply intrigued by the intersection of design and nature, constantly seeking ways to enhance life through thoughtful design principles. Let's connect and delve into the realms of UX, web design, and development, exploring opportunities for collaboration and knowledge sharing!
